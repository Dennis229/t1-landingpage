ABOUT T1

Rooted in our legacy of raising trophies and inspired by our passion for victory.

We are T1.

T1 Entertainment & Sports is a global esports joint venture that owns and operates the ultra-successful T1 League of Legends (LoL) Champions Korea (LCK) team, along with teams in competitive gaming segments that include Dota 2, Super Smash Bros., Valorant, Wild Rift, Overwatch Contenders, Splitgate. Building upon the rich legacy of SKT T1, T1 will expand its teams globally, celebrate new victories, and create even more opportunities for fans to embrace T1's gaming culture, content, and players in every corner of the world.

Accomplishments

League of Legends

2013

In 2012, SK Telecom expanded into the League of Legends esports scene by acquiring the roster of former esports organization 'Eat Sleep Game', and created the team SK Telecom T1 S. Then on Februarz 26, 2013 they created their second League of Legends team naming it, SK Telecom T1 K. While SK Telecom T1 S failed to qualify for the 2013 League of Legends World Championship, their counter part SK Telecom T1 K managed to secure a spot for the World Championship by Winning the 2013 League of Legends Champions Korea (LCK) Summer Season. And end up winning the 2013 League of Legends World Championship.

2014

SK Telecom T1 K continued their successfull start in the League of Legends esports scene by winning the 2014 Winter Season of League of Legends Champions Korea (LCK) without losing a single round. They would then go on to win All-Star Paris 2014. However, they couldn't transfer their Winter Success into the upcoming Spring and Summer Season, resulting in them failing to qualift for the 2014 World Championship.In the offseason, Riot Games changed the team regulations so that each organiztion maz onlz have one team participatin in each league. Which led to the merge of SKT T1 K and SKT T1 S. The new team would compete under the name SKT T1.

2015

Following the Merge of the 2 Rosters. SKT T1 had a spectatuclar 2015 Season, winning both Spring and Summer season of LCK and advancing in to the World Championship 2015. Which they managed to win while only losing one game in the finals for a record of 15-1, making SKT T1 the first team, in the history of competitive League of Legends, to win 2 World Championships.

2016

SKT T1 started of strong by winning the 2016 Spring season of LCK, this granted them a spot in the 2016 Mid-Season-Invitational (MSI). Which was the only major international tournament SKT T1 had yet to win. So they came in being a heavily favored team, but wwere initially unable to perfom up to their usual standards, which caues them to lose several games in the group stage. However, they managed to turn things around during the elimination round and eventually managed to win the 2016 Mid-Season-Invitational (MSI). While they managed to win MSI, SKT T1 placed third in the regular Summer season of LCK, but still qualifying for the 2016 World Championship. The team swept through the Group Stage and advanced further. In the end they advanced all the way to the finals and managed to win the 2016 World Championship in a close 3-2 Game, winning their third world championship title.

2017

In 2017 they started off strong again, winning the 2017 Spring season of LCK, granting them another spot in the MSI, they went to the group stage with an outstanding score of 8-2. Advancing to the finals by winning 3-0 in the semifinals. In the end they managed to win the 2017 MSI in an 3-1 series, becoming the first team to ever win the MSI back-to-back. They managed to qualify for the 2017 World Championship, and were considered a favourite to win the World Championship for a fourth time. They managed to fight their way to the finals again, but lost to the same opponent they faced the year before.

2019

In 2019, SKT T1 won the LCK Spring season further advancing to MSI 2019, but losing in the semifinals. But SKT T1 managed to defend their title and won the 2019 Summer Season of LCK, qualifying for the 2019 World Championship. But losing in the semifinals against the same opponent they lost to in MSI. In October 2019, SKT rebranded as T1.

2022

During the 2022 LCK Spring season, T1 went undefeated in the regular season with an 18-0 record, becoming the first team to achieve this record in the LCK. They managed to win the Season making it their tenth LCK title. On the International Stage they finished MSI and the World Champinship as Runners-up.
